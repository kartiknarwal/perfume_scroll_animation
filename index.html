Bold and magnetic, Dior Sauvage captures the spirit of raw freedom. Fresh bergamot blends with rugged amber and smoky woods, evoking endless skies and untamed horizons.

Gucci Flora is an ode to modern femininity. Soft peonies and jasmine bloom over warm sandalwood, creating a fragrance that feels graceful, radiant, and effortlessly elegant.

Oud Al Sultan whispers royalty in every note. Deep oud mingles with golden amber and exotic spices, weaving a scent that speaks of power, tradition, and timeless luxury.

Rasasi Hawas defines energy and confidence. Crisp citrus, refreshing aquatic notes, and warm musk collide, leaving behind a trail of bold freshness and lasting charm.

Tom Ford stands for refined seduction. Dark vanilla, rich tobacco, and hints of leather unfold slowly, creating an aura that’s mysterious, elegant, and irresistibly sensual.

Thank you for exploring our world of fragrances. Each scent is more than a perfume — it’s a story, a feeling, a memory reborn. May these aromas linger with you, inspiring confidence, elegance, and the art of being unforgettable.
